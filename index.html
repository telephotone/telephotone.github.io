ENSEMBLE ── how to build harmony
  • Multiple receivers on one light: set Transpose A=±0 / B=+4 / C=+7 for a major triad from a single note
  • Tens-of-ms timing spread between devices is embraced as ensemble looseness
  • Keep only the emitting surface inside the ROI; fix the camera on a tripod
  • Re-capture calibration whenever lighting conditions change
  • Camera requires https (or localhost)
  • Tough conditions: set Scale to DIATONIC 15 or PENTA 11 on both TX and RX ── level gaps widen 1.7×/2.3× while keeping the full 2-octave range
  • iPhone: turn OFF the side silent switch ── Web Audio is muted while the ringer is silenced
⚠ DO NOT STARE INTO THE EMITTING SURFACE. Output is mostly steady luminance, but calibration light involves blinking. Take care if you are sensitive to flashing lights.
