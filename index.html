ENSEMBLE ── how to build harmony
  • One blinking light, many receivers: every phone that decodes the score plays it. Set Transpose A=±0 / B=+4 / C=+7 for parallel harmony
  • Binary blink (bright/dark) is far more robust than luminance levels ── works in daylight-ish conditions and at distance
  • Keep only the emitting surface inside the ROI; fix the camera
  • 2 BPS is the surest (MABATAKI-proven). Raise the rate only when the waveform looks cleanly square
  • Camera requires https (or localhost)
  • iPhone: turn OFF the side silent switch ── Web Audio is muted while the ringer is silenced
⚠ DO NOT STARE INTO THE EMITTING SURFACE. Score transmission uses rapid blinking light. Take care if you are sensitive to flashing lights.
